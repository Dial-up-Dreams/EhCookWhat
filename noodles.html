üç≥ Noodle Cooking Tips

Choose the Right Noodle

Different dishes call for different noodles. Use egg noodles for chow mein, wheat noodles for dan dan, and rice noodles for pad thai.

Cook Al Dente

Cook noodles 1-2 minutes less than the package suggests. They'll continue cooking when you stir-fry them.

Get Your Wok Hot

A hot wok is essential for authentic flavors. Let it heat for 2-3 minutes before adding oil and ingredients.

Oil Your Noodles

Toss cooked noodles with a bit of oil to prevent clumping. This keeps them from sticking together.

Prep Everything First

Noodle cooking is fast! Have all ingredients chopped and ready (mise en place) before you start cooking.

Balance Your Flavors

A good noodle dish has balance: salty (soy sauce), sweet (sugar/hoisin), sour (vinegar), and spicy (chili).
